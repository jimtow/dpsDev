my500mERG enables personal workout metrics for any erg and settings. my500mErg is a simple calculator for a 500m split time and a workout at any cadence. It is based on the concept of a sustainable full-effort (FE) with a sustainable cadence (SC) for the rower. The rower inputs their watts, 500m split time, sustainable cadence, and estimated distance per stroke. The calculator then computes the distance per stroke and watts for a workout with any cadence and duration. It also includes a timer for tracking the workout duration.
