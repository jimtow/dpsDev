my500mERG enables personal Row! metrics for any erg and settings. my500mErg is a simple calculator for a 500m split time and a workout at any cadence. It is based on the concept of a sustainable full-effort (FE) with a sustainable cadence (SC) for the rower. The rower estimates FE watts, 500m split time, sustainable cadence, and and an estimated distance per stroke. my500mErg then computes the distance per stroke and watts for a workout with any cadence and duration. m5500mErg also includes a timer for tracking the workout duration. my500mErg is designed to be user-friendly and accessible
