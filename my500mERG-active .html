my500mERG provides On-the-Water equivalent metrics for any Erg and settings based on the concept of a rower's sustainable full-effort (FE) and cadence (SC). The rower estimates FE watts, 500m split time, and sustainable cadence. my500mErg then computes the On-the-Water equivalent distance per stroke and watts for a workout with any cadence and duration. my 500mErg also includes a timer for tracking the cadence and workout duration. my500mErg is designed to be user-friendly and accessible 0722.0938
